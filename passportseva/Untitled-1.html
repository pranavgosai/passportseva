Passport Apply Online In India

Passports are a necessity when a person is traveling within or outside the country. As per the Mission Mode Project launched by the government of India, the focus has been on electronic governance and providing e-facilities in various sectors. Under the Mission Mode Project, e-Passport services are now rendered and it has successfully helped in solving the Passport application problem to a great extent. This article focuses on the procedure to apply for a passport in India.

What is a Passport?

A passport is a compulsory document that is required when you are traveling outside the country. People travel for different purposes and they need passports as a verification document. As more and more people are opting for air travel, there is a growing need for more passports and hence, the Indian government launched a Passport Seva portal for an easy resolution to all passport-related queries. Currently, there are 36 passport offices around the country. Also, applicants can apply for a passport through our website Passport-sahayata.org

Types of Passport

1.Blue Passport- The most ordinary kind of passport issued for civilians and is blue.

2.White Passport- These passports are issued to government officials when they go abroad for their official work.

3.Maroon Passport- Also called Diplomats; Passport, these passports are issued to the high-rank officials of the government of India.
